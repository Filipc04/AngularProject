My top 3 animes

What I really enjoyed about this anime was its raw, emotional storytelling and complex characters. It delves into themes like revenge, honor, and the search for meaning in a violent world. The historical Viking setting is beautifully realized, and the intense battles are balanced with thought-provoking philosophical moments that make you question the cycle of hatred. I really recommend this anime, you can watch it's trailer here.

What makes Mob Psycho 100 so special is its perfect balance of humor, heart, and mind-blowing action sequences. Mob is such an endearing protagonist—his struggle to become a better person despite his overwhelming psychic powers is both relatable and inspiring. The animation is wild and experimental, making every fight scene a visual spectacle. But beyond the flashy exterior, it’s the quiet moments of vulnerability and the genuine relationships between the characters that truly make it unforgettable. Trailer.

One of the things I loved most about Hunter x Hunter is how it constantly subverts expectations. What starts as a seemingly lighthearted adventure quickly evolves into a layered and morally complex story. The world-building is unparalleled, with each arc introducing unique settings, challenges, and characters. The dynamic between Gon and Killua is the heart of the series, and their growth, both individually and together, is beautifully portrayed. Whether it’s the intellectual depth of Nen or the emotional weight of its darker moments, this anime keeps you thinking long after it’s over. See the trailer here.
